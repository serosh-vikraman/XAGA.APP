About XAGA

Based in Bangalore, XAGA is a forward-thinking manufacturer committed to redefining electric motorcycles through a fusion of cutting-edge design and advanced engineering. The company’s flagship model, the VIGILANTE 5K, has been purposefully engineered as a premier urban mobility solution.

"The shift toward electric mobility isn't just a change in fuel; it’s a total redesign of how we move."

Here is a rephrased look at the themes driving XAGA's innovation:

Rising Demand for Sustainable Mobility

The accelerating pivot toward electric two-wheelers highlights a global appetite for eco-friendly, efficient urban transport. XAGA has met this challenge by developing solutions that align with the modern commuter’s environmental and performance expectations.

Patented Monocoque Chassis Innovation

By leveraging a proprietary monocoque chassis design, XAGA has redefined structural integrity. This engineering breakthrough allows for the integration of lightweight, high-carbon steel, significantly enhancing vehicle durability, agility, and long-term performance.

High-Performance Powertrains

To satisfy the market’s demand for power, XAGA has engineered advanced Hub Motors. These powertrains are designed to bridge the gap between efficiency and excitement, offering riders higher top speeds and significantly extended ranges.
